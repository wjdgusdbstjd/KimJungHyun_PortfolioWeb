Hello, my name is Kim Jeonghyeon, and I am striving to become a skilled UI/UX designer. I find it incredibly fascinating to imagine the entire process a user goes through when interacting with a service and to think deeply about how to provide an even better user experience. This passion is what motivated me to apply for the advanced major program. During my second year in the associate degree program, I had the opportunity to work on various team projects, where we collaboratively planned services and visually expressed them through design. Additionally, creating promotional websites for these services deepened my interest in UI/UX. Through the advanced major program, I aim to become a UI/UX designer who combines both practical skills and theoretical knowledge. By leveraging the programming skills I acquired during my associate degree, I am confident in creating diverse portfolios that will help me grow into a more competitive designer. I pride myself on my quick decision-making and ability to generate fresh ideas, which allow me to work efficiently. I also enjoy exploring diverse perspectives and incorporating them into my work. With these strengths, I aspire to create unique and impactful results that showcase my individuality.
